📉 Impact of Cyber Attacks

👤 Account Takeover: Attackers gain full control of user accounts.
📂 Data Theft: Sensitive personal information can be stolen.
🔓 Privacy Breach: Private user data and activity may be exposed.
💸 Financial Loss: In real systems, attackers can misuse banking or payment data.
🏷️ Reputation Damage: Users lose trust in the platform or system.
